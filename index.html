About Us

Nakshatra is a the mathematical and astronomy society of NSUT. In Hindu astrology, Nakshatras are the 27 lunar mansions that divide the ecliptic into 27 equal parts. Each Nakshatra is associated with a particular star or constellation. As the name suggest this society is for those who are space and Mathematics enthusiasts.
